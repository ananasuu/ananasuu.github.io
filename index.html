Nina Friedrich

What started as simply putting together matching clothes quickly turned into creating fully self-made outfits complete with magical props. Since 2022, I've been diving deep into crafting my own cosplays from scratch, bringing characters to life in my own way — all while starting my career as a software developer shortly after.

About my Work

Cosplays & Buildbooks

Here you'll find all my costumes! If you'd like to do a photoshoot together, just send me a DM on Instagram or an email — I’d love to hear from you!

Whenever possible, I’ve linked my build books so you can see how I brought each costume to life. All of them were created in Canva and are available through Google Drive — I hope they inspire your own projects!

  • Marcille Donato

    Delicious in Dungeon

    Silly, Powerful & Hungry!

    Marcille Donato is a character from a story about adventurers cooking and eating monsters. I made this cosplay for the 2025 S.a.m.t Con cosplay contest and was thrilled to win 1st place.

    Build Book

  • Frieren

    Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

    Adventurous, Wise & Kind!

    Frieren is a character from a story about an elf mage who travels to understand humanity. I competed with this cosplay at the 2024 Bijutsu contest and won Best in Craftsmanship.

    Build Book

  • Laudna

    Critical Role

    Dark, Spooky & Fun!

    Laudna is a character from Critical Role, a D&D show. I made this cosplay for the 2024 Dokomi contest and placed 3rd.

  • Tohru

    Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid

    Cute, Outgoing & Funny!

    Tohru is a dragon mastering her life with her savior Miss Kobayashi. This slice of life anime is a pure comfort show.
